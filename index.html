Our Story

About Us

The K Woods Foundation was founded in August 2014, in memory of Mr Kenny Woods, by his son Adam, to raise funds and awareness in order to improve the lives of people living with Dementia within the Wirral area.

The Take That Experience

A annually held event at The Floral Pavilion in New Brighton. A night of singing and entertainment for all ages. A variety of local acts get the night going followed by The Take That Experience to round of the event.

Black and White Ball

A brand new event for the Foundation. Guests are invited to attend for a luxurious three course meal and evening of entertainment at the fabulous Thornton Hall Hotel on the Wirral.
